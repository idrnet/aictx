## Summary

Which EU neobanks allow free (or cheap) top-up from credit cards + free instant SEPA transfers? Useful for bridging cash flow gaps using available credit card limits.

Since the **2025 EU Instant Payments Regulation**, all EU banks must offer instant SEPA at no extra cost vs regular SEPA. The differentiator is now **free CC top-up**.

## bunq Top-Up Limits (Detail)

Hard limits regardless of plan:

| Account age | Per 24h | Per 7 days | Per 30 days |
|-------------|---------|------------|-------------|
| **< 1 month** | 500€ | 800€ | 1.200€ |
| **> 1 month** | 500€ | 1.500€ | 2.000€ |

- Free allowance: first 500€/month — no fee
- After 500€: 0,5% (EEA cards) or 2,5% (non-EEA cards)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay top-ups **count toward the same monthly limit**
- Paid plans (Core/Pro/Elite) do **not** increase top-up limits — they add other features

## Apple Pay Top-Up Notes

- Apple Pay and Google Pay top-ups use the linked credit card — same fees apply
- Top-up is instant (same as regular CC top-up)
- Both bunq and Vivid confirm: digital wallet top-ups count toward monthly CC limits
- Curve card can be added to Apple Pay to route any credit card through it

## AEAT Embargo Risk (Foreign IBAN)

- Spanish AEAT embargos primarily target Spanish banks via direct notification
- EU regulation (SEPA) requires equal treatment of all EU IBANs, but enforcement differs
- In practice, AEAT has **limited direct seizure capability** on foreign IBANs (DE, NL, LT)
- For high-value debts, AEAT can use EU mutual assistance directives — but this is slow and rare
- A German/Dutch IBAN adds **procedural friction**, it does **not** make you immune
- Use foreign IBANs for cash flow management, not for avoiding obligations

## Square POS Payouts

- Square Spain requires a **Spanish bank account (ES IBAN)** for merchant payouts
- German/Dutch IBANs from neobanks are **not accepted** for Square merchant settlement
- Workaround: receive Square payouts to Spanish bank → then transfer to neobank

## Best Options by Use Case

- **Bridge cash flow (CC → bank → pay bills):** Revolut (free) + bunq (500€ extra free)
- **Higher volume CC top-up (>1.000€/mo):** bunq Free (lowest fee at 0,5%)
- **Stay within free tier:** Revolut (~1.000€) + Vivid Standard (200€) + bunq (500€) = ~1.700€/mo free
- **Apple Pay convenience:** Vivid or bunq (both support, counts toward CC limit)
- **Embargo friction:** Vivid (DE IBAN) or bunq (NL IBAN)
